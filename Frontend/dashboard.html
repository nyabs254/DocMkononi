Remember!! The 8 Doctors of Health (NEWSTART)

Nutrition

Eat balanced meals rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and natural foods to nourish and strengthen your body.

Exercise

Aim for moderate intensity physical activity (like brisk walking or gardening) for at least 150 minutes a week, with muscle-strengthening activities 2 days a week.

Water

Drink enough clean water daily to stay hydrated, boost metabolism, and support vital body functions.

Sunlight

Get safe exposure to sunlight to promote vitamin D production and improve mood and immune function.

Temperance

Practice moderation and avoid harmful substances to maintain balance and protect your long-term health.

Air

Breathe fresh air and practice deep breathing to improve oxygen flow and mental clarity.

Rest

Ensure adequate sleep and relaxation to restore energy, repair cells, and maintain emotional balance.

Trust in God

Cultivate spiritual faith and inner peace to strengthen resilience and promote holistic well-being.
